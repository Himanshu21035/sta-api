┌────────────── second (optional)
│ ┌──────────── minute (0-59)
│ │ ┌────────── hour (0-23)
│ │ │ ┌──────── day of month (1-31)
│ │ │ │ ┌────── month (1-12)
│ │ │ │ │ ┌──── day of week (0-6, Sunday-Saturday)
│ │ │ │ │ │
* * * * * *

'0 0 1 * *'  → Midnight on 1st of every month
'0 9 1 * *'  → 9 AM on 1st of every month
'0 0 1 1 *'  → Midnight on Jan 1st only
